Our Story

Raha was born from a simple belief — that food should bring people peace, joy, and a sense of home. Founded in Nyali, Mombasa in 2010 as Prem Restaurant, we set out to serve high-quality, flavourful meals in a warm and welcoming space, making every guest feel like family.

From our signature broasted chicken to our fresh-made sides, every dish is prepared with care, using the finest ingredients and time-honoured recipes passed down through generations.
